Crisis Help — You Are Not Alone

Call Befrienders Kenya: +254 722 178 177 Kenya Red Cross: 1199 EMKF Crisis Helpline: +254 800 723 253

Warning Signs You Might Be in Crisis

  • Thinking about harming yourself or suicidal thoughts
  • Feeling completely overwhelmed or unsafe
  • Inability to cope or calm yourself

What You Can Do Right Now

  1. Take slow, deep breaths and ground yourself.
  2. Reach out to someone you trust—parent, friend, or mentor.
  3. Use a grounding technique (e.g. five things you can see, five things you can touch).
  4. If the situation feels urgent, please call one of the numbers above immediately.

Local Support Services

Befrienders Kenya: Emotional support and suicide prevention via call, SMS or WhatsApp: +254 722 178 177

Kenya Red Cross Society: Toll-free mental health and crisis support: 1199
